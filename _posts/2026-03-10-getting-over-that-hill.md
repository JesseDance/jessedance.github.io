## Breaking the Ice

This is my attempt at building the habit of writing for the public. Writing has been a particular struggle for myself, as it likely is for pretty much anyone that attempts it, but I have heard that practice and repitition is the only remedy. This is not to say that it ever gets easy, just that the mental muscles get stronger. All this to say that I hope my writing is a more pleasant experience in the near future.
